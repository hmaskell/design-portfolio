I'm Heather

I have a BSc in Interactive Arts and Technology with a concentration in Interactive Systems. I've completed two UX internships at Microsoft and Evident Point Software.

A dream job for me would be to work on a product I believe in with people who are equally excited about collaborating to improve it for those who use it.

If you're looking for someone to empathize with users, reimagine digital services as an intuitive, attractive and delightful experience, and collaborate candidly with the team to include feedback and transform designs into code - I'm your girl!
